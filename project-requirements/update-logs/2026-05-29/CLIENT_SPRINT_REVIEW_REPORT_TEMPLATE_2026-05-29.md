## Summary

Created a reusable client-facing sprint review report template for post-sprint communication. The template is business-facing and concise, with emphasis on completed outcomes, business impact, launch-plan position, remaining client-relevant issues, and next sprint direction.

---

## Template Structure Added

- Document Control with sprint, report, source-plan, and product-environment metadata.
- Executive Summary limited to concise business-facing bullets.
- Launch Plan Position Snapshot using a Mermaid launch progress track plus readiness gauge instead of duplicating the sprint calendar table.
- Sprint Outcomes table combining planned focus, completed work, business impact, and client action.
- Open Items, Risks & Next Sprint Direction table combining remaining concerns, handling plan, and client input.

Same-day revision: strengthened the Launch Plan Position Snapshot so it is more visually digestible for clients, with a stage-based progress track and launch-readiness gauge rather than a plain linear sprint list.

---

## Communication Rules

The template is designed to avoid internal developer backlog detail. It excludes reproduction steps, Plane ticket IDs, internal implementation ownership, estimates, and full sprint-planning content.
