# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Hairline Ltd ("Hairline", "we", "us", or "our") operates a digital platform that helps patients connect with hair transplant providers, request and compare quotes, make bookings and payments, receive aftercare support, and contact support teams through the Hairline mobile app, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Because Hairline handles personal data, health-related information, treatment media, support communications, and payment-related records, we apply heightened privacy and security controls across the platform.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, how long we keep it, and the choices available to you.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

- the Hairline patient mobile application;
- Hairline websites and web pages that link to this Privacy Policy;
- support interactions, account management, and deletion requests handled by Hairline; and
- related provider, booking, payment, and aftercare workflows operated through the Hairline platform.

This Privacy Policy does not replace the privacy practices of independent clinics, doctors, hospitals, or other healthcare providers that may use Hairline. When you engage directly with a provider, that provider may also have its own privacy notice and legal obligations.

## 3. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated when you use the Services, and limited information from service providers or partners involved in delivering the Services.

### 3.1 Account and profile information

We may collect:

- name;
- email address;
- phone number;
- date of birth;
- gender, city, country, and profile details;
- account credentials and verification records; and
- profile photo or avatar, if you choose to upload one.

### 3.2 Inquiry, booking, and treatment information

We may collect information you submit when using Hairline to find and manage treatment services, including:

- preferred destinations, providers, and treatment dates;
- inquiry details, treatment interests, and service preferences;
- quote comparison, booking, and scheduling information;
- treatment-related notes and status information; and
- travel or identity documents you submit through the platform, such as passport details, where required for a booking or related support workflow.

### 3.3 Health and medical-related information

Because Hairline is used in connection with hair restoration treatment, we may process health-related or sensitive information you choose to provide or generate through the Services, including:

- medical questionnaire responses;
- treatment history or concerns;
- head scan photo sets, treatment photos, recovery photos, and uploaded media;
- aftercare questionnaire responses, milestone tracking, and medication-related information; and
- support or safety communications relating to treatment or recovery.

We request that you provide only information that is reasonably necessary for the Services.

### 3.4 Payment and transaction information

We may collect and store:

- payment status, transaction identifiers, booking amounts, refund records, and billing metadata; and
- limited information returned by our payment processors to confirm payment status or reconcile transactions.

Payment card details are generally processed by our payment providers, including Stripe, rather than stored in full by Hairline.

### 3.5 Support and communications data

If you contact us or submit a support request, we may collect:

- support case titles, categories, priorities, and descriptions;
- attachments you upload;
- support ticket history and status;
- messages exchanged with Hairline support staff; and
- feedback and feature requests you send us.

### 3.6 Technical, device, and usage information

We may collect technical and operational data such as:

- IP address;
- app, browser, and device information;
- authentication and security logs;
- crash, error, and diagnostic data;
- notification preferences and settings changes; and
- audit trail records associated with account, security, support, and legal-content actions.

## 4. How We Use Information

We use personal data only as reasonably necessary to operate, secure, improve, and support the Services. This includes using information to:

- create, authenticate, and manage your account;
- verify your identity for sensitive actions, including account deletion requests;
- route inquiries to relevant providers and support quote comparison, booking, payment, and aftercare workflows;
- show treatment progress, recovery content, and support history inside the platform;
- respond to support requests, complaints, disputes, and safety-related issues;
- send transactional communications such as OTPs, booking updates, payment updates, support case notifications, and deletion-request status updates;
- maintain platform security, detect fraud or misuse, and enforce our Terms of Use;
- comply with medical, financial, tax, audit, dispute-resolution, and legal obligations; and
- improve the reliability and usability of the platform through service analytics and operational review.

We do not use health-related data for advertising profiling, and we do not sell your health-related data.

## 5. When We Share Information

We share information only where needed to operate the platform, deliver the Services you request, comply with the law, or protect users and Hairline.

### 5.1 With treatment providers

We may share relevant information with clinics, doctors, hospitals, or provider teams when that sharing is needed to:

- review and respond to your inquiry;
- prepare quotes or treatment plans;
- manage bookings, treatment logistics, or aftercare; or
- respond to treatment-related support or safety concerns.

### 5.2 With service providers

We may share information with service providers acting on our behalf, including providers that support:

- payment processing;
- secure hosting and storage;
- identity verification and authentication;
- customer support tooling;
- notifications and transactional messaging; and
- platform security, monitoring, or backup operations.

These providers are expected to process personal data only for authorized purposes and under appropriate safeguards.

### 5.3 With support, compliance, and internal operations teams

Authorized Hairline personnel may access information where needed to:

- resolve support cases;
- investigate abuse, fraud, safety, payment, or account issues;
- manage deletion and retention requests; or
- maintain audit, compliance, and legal records.

### 5.4 For legal or safety reasons

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, court order, or lawful government request, or if disclosure is necessary to:

- protect patient safety or the safety of others;
- investigate fraud, abuse, or security incidents;
- enforce our legal rights; or
- support dispute resolution and compliance requirements.

### 5.5 Business transfers

If Hairline is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, restructuring, or sale of assets, personal data may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to applicable law and appropriate safeguards.

## 6. Data Retention

Hairline applies retention rules designed for healthcare- and finance-adjacent operations.

### 6.1 Standard retention rules

Hairline retains:

- medical and treatment-related records for a minimum of 7 years;
- financial transaction records for a minimum of 7 years; and
- audit logs for a minimum of 7 years.

Some support records, booking records, messages, and media may also be retained where they are part of treatment, payment, dispute, or audit history.

### 6.2 Backup retention

Hairline system backups are designed to run regularly and may retain deleted data for limited backup-recovery periods. Backup retention may last for up to 30 days.

### 6.3 Deletion and anonymization

When a verified deletion request is approved, Hairline will delete or anonymize personal data that it is not legally required to keep. Where we must retain records for legal, medical, financial, security, or audit reasons, we will restrict access and keep only what is required.

## 7. Account Deletion and Data Rights

You may request deletion of your account and eligible personal data. Hairline's current platform requirements provide that:

- verified deletion requests are typically completed within 30 calendar days;
- additional verification may be required for security or legal reasons;
- deletion may be limited, delayed, or scoped differently if you have active treatment, aftercare, payment, safety, or legal obligations;
- some data may be retained where the law or a legitimate compliance obligation requires retention; and
- you will be informed if certain records cannot be fully deleted and why.

For detailed instructions, see our [Account Deletion](./account-deletion.md) page.

Depending on your location, you may also have rights to request access, correction, or restriction of certain personal data. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

## 8. Security

Hairline applies technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect sensitive information, including:

- encryption at rest and in transit;
- role-based access controls;
- audit logging for protected records and sensitive actions;
- authentication and verification controls for account access and destructive actions; and
- operational monitoring, backup, and incident-response processes.

No system can guarantee absolute security. You should also keep your password and device secure and notify us promptly if you believe your account has been compromised.

## 9. International Use

Hairline may operate across countries and may involve providers, staff, vendors, or infrastructure located in more than one jurisdiction. Where personal data is transferred internationally, we will use appropriate safeguards required by applicable law.

## 10. Children

Hairline is intended for people seeking hair restoration services and is not designed for young children. If you believe a child has provided personal data to Hairline inappropriately, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

## 11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the Services, legal requirements, or privacy practices change. When required, we will publish the updated version, update the effective date, and prompt users to review or re-accept material changes through the platform.

## 12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your personal data, or a privacy or deletion request, contact:

- Support email: support@hairline.com
- Support page: [Contact Support](./contact-support.md)

If you are experiencing an urgent medical issue, do not rely on email or in-app support. Contact your treating provider or local emergency services immediately.
