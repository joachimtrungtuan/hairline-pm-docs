# Account Deletion

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your Hairline account and eligible personal data.

## 1. Your options to request deletion

### Option A: Request deletion in the app

If you can still access your Hairline account, open the patient profile area and select:

- `Delete account`

You may be asked to re-authenticate with your password or a one-time code before the request is submitted.

### Option B: Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email:

- <support@hairline.com>

Use the subject line:

- `Account Deletion Request`

For security, please send the request from the email address registered to your Hairline account whenever possible.

## 2. What happens after you submit a request

After Hairline receives your request:

1. we confirm receipt of the request;
2. we may ask you to verify your identity;
3. we review whether any active treatment, aftercare, payment, safety, fraud, or legal obligations affect the request;
4. we delete or anonymize eligible personal data where allowed; and
5. we notify you when the request is completed or if some records must be retained.

Verified deletion requests are typically completed within 30 calendar days. If more verification is needed, or if a legal or safety review is required, the timeline may extend or pause until the required information is received.

## 3. When deletion may be delayed, limited, or unavailable

Hairline may not be able to complete deletion immediately if:

- you have active treatment or aftercare obligations;
- you have a payment or billing matter in progress;
- records must be retained for legal, medical, tax, fraud-prevention, safety, or audit reasons; or
- additional identity verification is required to protect your account.

If a request cannot be completed in full, Hairline will explain what data is being retained and why.

## 4. What Hairline may delete or anonymize

Subject to applicable law and platform obligations, Hairline may delete or anonymize data such as:

- account profile information;
- preferences and non-essential settings;
- eligible support and communication records;
- uploaded media that is not legally required to be retained; and
- other personal data that is no longer needed for an active service or compliance obligation.

## 5. What Hairline may retain

Hairline may retain some records even after an account deletion request is approved. This may include:

- medical or treatment-related records retained for at least 7 years;
- financial transaction and billing records retained for at least 7 years;
- audit logs retained for at least 7 years; and
- backup data that may remain for up to 30 days before backup rotation removes it.

Retained records are kept only where required or justified by legal, safety, compliance, fraud-prevention, or dispute-resolution obligations, and access should be restricted accordingly.

## 6. Important notes for healthcare-related use

Because Hairline is used in connection with medical treatment workflows:

- account deletion does not erase provider-side medical obligations;
- urgent care issues must still be handled through your treating provider or local emergency services; and
- some treatment, payment, and support records may need to be preserved even if your account is closed.

## 7. Questions

If you have questions about account deletion, privacy, or retained records, contact:

- <support@hairline.com>

You can also review our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md) and [Contact Support](./contact-support.md) page.
