# Contact Support

Last updated: March 11, 2026

Hairline support is available to help with account access, quote and booking questions, payment and billing issues, support cases, app issues, and general platform guidance.

## 1. Contact options

### In-app support

If you have a Hairline account, the fastest way to contact us for account-specific help is through the Hairline app or platform support flow. This lets us:

- link your request to your account securely;
- review your support history and attachments;
- send updates through your case thread; and
- track the status of your request more accurately.

### Email support

If you cannot access the app or need help before signing in, email:

- support@hairline.com

When possible, send your message from the email address registered to your Hairline account.

## 2. Support hours

Current support hours are:

- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
- Saturday: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
- Sunday: Closed

## 3. Expected response times

Hairline aims to acknowledge standard support requests within 24 hours during normal support operations. Some issues may take longer if they involve provider coordination, payment review, identity verification, medical-safety escalation, or legal/compliance review.

## 4. What to include in your message

To help us resolve your issue faster, include:

- your full name;
- the email address linked to your Hairline account;
- a short description of the issue;
- any relevant booking, quote, case, or payment reference if available; and
- screenshots or documents that help explain the problem.

Please do not send unnecessary medical or identity documents by email if a secure in-app workflow is available for that purpose.

## 5. Types of issues we can help with

You can contact Hairline support for matters such as:

- login or verification problems;
- account and profile issues;
- quote, booking, and scheduling questions;
- payment or billing questions;
- help using Hairline features;
- support case follow-up;
- general feedback or bug reports; and
- account deletion requests or privacy questions.

## 6. Medical and emergency issues

Hairline support is not an emergency medical service.

If you believe you are experiencing:

- a medical emergency;
- a severe or rapidly worsening post-treatment complication; or
- a situation where urgent clinical care is needed,

contact your treating provider or local emergency services immediately. Do not rely on email or support-case response times for urgent medical care.

## 7. Privacy and security

When you contact us, we may collect and use your support messages, attachments, and case history to investigate and resolve your request, keep an audit trail, and comply with legal obligations. For more information, see our [Privacy Policy](./privacy-policy.md).

## 8. Account deletion requests

If you want to delete your Hairline account, see our [Account Deletion](./account-deletion.md) page for instructions and timelines.
